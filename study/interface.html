WHY IS THIS STUDY BEING DONE?

We aim to reproduce the human evaluation of several Natural Language Processing pipelines for paraphrase generation.

WHAT WILL I BE ASKED TO DO DURING THE STUDY?

You will be asked to choose which system performs better between two paraphrases created by a computer program. To do so, you need to read roughly thirty examples of sentences and two paraphrases created by a computer program and determine which system performs better in three aspects, including fluency, meaning, and dissimilarity.

To be more specific, we would like you to answer__:

  1. Which system output is the most fluent and grammatical?

  2. To what extent is the meaning expressed in the original sentence preserved in the rewritten version, with no additional information added?

  3. Does the rewritten version use different words or phrasing to the original? You should choose the system that uses the most different words or word order.

HOW MUCH TIME WILL I SPEND ON THE STUDY?

Each task takes 20 minutes to complete.

Instructions

In this task you will read roughly thirty examples of sentences and two paraphrases created by a computer program. The program aims to rewrite the sentence so that it means the same thing, but using different words and/or different word order.

Please read all the sentences carefully, this should take you about 8 minutes (if you do the task very quickly your response will be rejected).

You will be asked to choose which system performs better, for the below aspect of the paraphrases:

  • To what extent is the meaning expressed in the original sentence preserved in the rewritten version, with no additional information added?

Remember that you are being asked to rate the system, not the original.

If the control samples are not answered correctly then we will assume that you have answered at random and reject the response.

A small number of samples may have two choices that are exactly the same - in these cases please pick an answer at random, this will not cause the response to be rejected.

Examples

First, complete these example tasks correctly:

Original: Who is the President of America?



System A:   "The Head of State of the USA is is whom?"

System B:   "Who is Captain America?"
Closest in meaning

System A is closer to the original meaning that System B, so click 'A'.

Original: Who is the President of America?



System A:   "Vice President of the USA is whom?"

System B:   "Who the President of America America?"
Closest in meaning

System B means the same thing as the original, but System A doesn't, so click 'B'.

Selected answers are incorrect, You must pick the correct answer in order to proceed!
